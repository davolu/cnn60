Happy 60th Birthday, Pastor Njoku Chibuike Noel

Fondly called Chike, alias CNN was born in Egbu-Owerri on 20th December, 1963 to the family of late Mr. Eugene Njoku and Mrs. Mercy Njoku both of Egbu in Owerri North local Government Area in Imo-State, Nigeria. He is the sixth child in a family of eight children and known to be the time keeper of his home as he never went to school or any activity late except it was outside his control.

CNN @ 60

CNN is an intelligent, dependable, loving, reliable and honest man with a high level of integrity, discipline and hardwork. He is a highly dedicated, resourceful, detailed and result oriented person and possesses excellent analytical and interpersonal skills. He is a lover of God and his people; and made Jesus his Lord and friend several years ago while he was on campus as an undergraduate and has served fervently in different capacity in several fellowships and the Church of God. He is currently a Pastor with Gospel Power Ministry, Ogba, Lagos, Nigeria.

Pastor Chike grew up in Egbu and to the glory of God; he studied Accountancy and is currently a member of the Chartered Institute of Accountants (ICAN), and also a member of the Chartered Institute of Taxation (CITN) and Chartered Institute of Pension (ACP) respectively. He has a Master’s degree in Business Administration and has attended several courses both in Nigeria and outside the country.

He worked with the Nigeria Biscuits
(Diamond Foods Ltd Ibadan) during his youth service between 1988 and 1989.

Thereafter, he started his career with Unipetrol Nigeria Plc which was later bought over by Oando Plc. He grew through the ranks in Oando and became the Company Treasurer before resigning to start his own business in 2013.

He is happily married to Pastor (Mrs) Njoku Tamunobubelebara Patricia and they are blessed with three wonderful biological children and several adopted children. Please join me and appreciate the Almighty God for His faithfulness; Grace, Mercy and Love over Pastor Chike Noel Njoku as he celebrates Sixty years of God's Goodness.
